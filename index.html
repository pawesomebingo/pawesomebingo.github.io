Our Mission

Our primary goal is to fundraise $500 or or more in funds to donate to Greenhill Humane Society (GHS) by hosting a bingo event named Pawesome Bingo (PB).

GHS is a life-saving animal shelter within the Eugene/Springfield area that has proudly been caring for animals in Lane County since 1944.

In 2025, GHS had: 2,833 animals adopted, 3,720 pets returned to owners, and 1.089 surgeries performed.

Who We Are

We are Lane Community College (LCC) students who are proudly enrolled in the new Applied Business Management (ABM) program that began in the Fall of '24.

The program is the first of its kind, offering anyone a chance to earn a bachelor's degree in just two years via community college in the United States.

Our Backstory

The awesome students behind Pawesome Bingo (PB) were very impressed with the local non-profit named Greenhill Humane Society (GHS).

GHS already gives back to the local community and the students felt the need to return the favor to both GHS and the local community. And thus PB was created to generate funds and spread awareness for GHS.

Event Details

The venue for Pawesome Bingo (PB) will be held at the Springfield Moose Lodge.

The lodge belongs to Moose International (MI), a Moose fraterinity and international organization of men and women dedicated to the caring of young and old, bringing communities closer together, and celebrating life

PB will be held on May 23rd, 2026 during 7:00 - 10:00 PM.
