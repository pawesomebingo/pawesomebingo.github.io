OUR MISSION

Pawesome Bingo's primary goal is to fundraise $1,500 or more funds to donate to Greenhill Humane Society by hosting a bingo fundraiser event.

Greenhill is a life-saving animal shelter within the Eugene/Springfield area that has proudly been caring for animals in Lane County since 1944.

In 2025, Greenhill had: 2,833 animals adopted, 3,720 pets returned to owners, and 1,089 surgeries performed.

The awesome students behind Pawesome Bingo were very impressed with Greenhill Humane Society and how they give back to the local community.
