The program offers anyone in Oregon a chance to earn a bachelor's degree in just two years and at a community college level of affordability.

OUR MISSION

Pawesome Bingo's primary goal is to fundraise $1,500 or more funds to donate to Greenhill Humane Society by hosting a bingo fundraiser event.

Greenhill is a life-saving animal shelter within the Eugene/Springfield area that has proudly been caring for animals in Lane County since 1944.

In 2025, Greenhill had: 2,833 animals adopted, 3,720 pets returned to owners, and 1,089 surgeries performed.

OUR EVENT

The Pawesome Bingo fundraiser event will be held at the Loyal Order of Moose in Springfield, Oregon (commonly called Moose Lodge or Bingo Hall).

The lodge belongs to Moose International, a Moose fraternity and international organization of men and women dedicated to the caring of young and old, bringing communities closer together, and the celebration of life.

OUR STORY

The awesome students behind Pawesome Bingo were very impressed with Greenhill Humane Society and how they give back to the local community.

Because of this, Pawesome felt the need to also give back to the local community by spreading awareness and gathering funds for Greenhill.
